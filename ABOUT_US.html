ABOUT US

My Journey

I had no idea I had a passion for baking until I made a cake for our family's Christmas Eve in the year of 2019. That is why my family encouraged me to turn this passion into a business. I prepared all the equipment and tools I would require to launch this firm. after many delays and hardships, I was able to acquire all of what I need and obtained my business permit to properly launch my firm thanks to my family's unwavering support.

It was difficult at first because not many people actually knew about my business, but thanks to my close relatives and friends who helped me out by supporting it and doing business with me, I was able to extensively grow my business in many locations here in Daet. Additionally, providing a service like delivering their purchases makes it much more appealing to our clients.

I have made baking my habit, passion, and business up to this point, and I intend to keep doing so since, in addition to bringing me income, baking also makes me happy when I bake and when I watch my clients enjoying my goodies.
